The Berlin Wall

Explore the story of the Berlin Wall—its rise, life, and fall. Discover the people, the events, and the impact that changed the world.

Introduction

The Berlin Wall stood from 1961 to 1989, dividing families, friends, and a nation. Built overnight, it became the starkest symbol of the Cold War—an emblem of oppression and a catalyst for hope, resistance, and eventual freedom.
